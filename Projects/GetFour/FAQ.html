Why do my custom emojis look wonky? Why do they sometimes not show up?

GetFour custom emojis work best when you use the Messenger-specific emojis. These are (supposedly) standard across all devices. If you use your device's custom native emojis (eg. Samsung emojis or iOS emojis), they may appear in a different size/shape than other Messenger-specific emojis.
Also, if you use your device's native emojis (for example, the Apple squirt-gun emoji), this may not appear correctly if you switch devices. If you play GetFour on your iPhone, then set an emoji to the squirt-gun iOS emoji, then later switch to your Android tablet or computer, the emoji may not show up at all.
One last note, iPads specifically have a wonky format when playing GetFour.
